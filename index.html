Space

Main Info

The term "Space" or "Outer Space" means the area around Earth. Outer space is very big, vast and full of galaxies and planets. Compared to outer space, we are just a speck of matter in a big galaxy.

(Fun Fact: There are about 20 Trillion galaxies in the Universe!)

Our Solar System

Our solar system is made out of 8 planets: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune (In Order). Our Sun is the star that gives us light, heat and energy. Jupiter is the biggest planet in the solar system, Venus is the hottest and Uranus is the coldest. Galaxies

Outer space is full of galaxies. We are in a galaxy called the Milky Way Galaxy. A galaxy is a cluster of millions and billlions of planets, stars and more held together by gravitational force.

Matter

Everything in outer space is made from matter. Matter is what we are made of, what the universe is made of, what everything is made of. Matter is universes to galaxies to planets to atoms and to subatomic particles. There are 4 types of matter: Solid, Liquid, Gas and Plasma. Solids have a definite shape and volume, liquid has a definite volume but not a definite shape as they take the shape of their container, gas has neither definite shape or volume and plasma is extremly heated gas.
